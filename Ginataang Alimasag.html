Ginataang Alimasag

Ginataang Alimasag (or alimango) are Crabs cooked in Coconut Milk with squash in it and make the dish savory and healthy.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 5 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 small red onion, chopped
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoons patis (fish sauce)
  • 2 medium-sized blue crabs (alimasag), washed, cut in half
  • 1/4 cup kalabasa (squash), peeled, cubed
  • 1/4 cup okra, halved
  • 2 pieces green finger chilies (siling pangsigang)
  • 1/4 cup sitaw (string beans), cut into 3-inch lengths
  • salt
  • ground black pepper

Procedure

Step 1: Heat cooking oil in a large pan. Add then saute onions and garlic until onions are translucent.

Step 2: Add coconut milk and patis, and bring to a boil. Cook for 6 minutes.

Step 3: Add the crabs and cook for 5 minutes.

Step 4: Add the squash and okra. Simmer until vegetables are tender. (You may also add the green finger chili at this point.) Add the string beans.

Step 5: Season it with salt and pepper.

Step 6: Serve and Satisfy your cravings!
