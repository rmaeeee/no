Tuyo Pasta

Tuyo pasta is a simple oil-based pasta made with flaked tuyo, garlic, tomatoes and chili flakes in olive oil. It is best served with garlic bread and topped with a lot of grated cheese.

Ingredients

  • 8 pieces tuyo
  • 4 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 4 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • pepper to taste
  • chili flakes to taste
  • 350 grams spaghetti pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 â€“ 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • cheese (grated)

Procedure

Step 1: Heat some oil in a pan. Fry the tuyo pieces for a minute until cooked. Using your hands, turn the tuyo into flakes. Set aside.

Step 2: Boil some water and cook the spaghetti pasta for 9 minutes.

Step 3: Melt butter in a pan and add the olive oil. Saute the minced garlic for a few minutes and then add the tomatoes and chili flakes. Stir a bit and then add the tuyo flakes.

Step 4: Add the cooked pasta and toss gently to coat with the sauce. Add some pasta water and toss again. Season with pepper.

Step 5: Transfer the tuyo pasta to a serving plate and top with a lot of grated cheese.

Step 5: Serve and Satisfy your cravings!
