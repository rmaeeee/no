Avocado Toast

Avocado Toast is a healthy breakfast because avocados are a fruit that is categorized as a fat. Most of the fat found in avocados is "good" monounsaturated fats. Because fat takes the body longer to digest, it makes you feel more satisfied after eating it.

Ingredients

  • ½ small avocado
  • ½ teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 slice bread
  • ½ teaspoon olive oil
  • red pepper flakes

Procedure

Step 1: In a small bowl, combine avocado, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Gently mash with the back of a fork.

Step 2: Toast the slice bread in bread toaster

Step 3: Top toasted bread with mashed avocado mixture. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with red pepper flakes

Step 4: Serve and Satisfy your cravings!
